The Designer

Before I wrote production code, I spent 18 months as a Graphic Designer at Meyiswa Training Academy (July 2022 – January 2024). Course materials, brochures, social assets, branded collateral. It was not portfolio work — it was design under deadline for real readers. That's where I learned typography, hierarchy, and the habit of designing for a specific audience rather than for my own taste.

Today I run design output across every Skhokho Labs build — from the product UI of Skhokho itself to client-facing decks, websites, and brand work. Design and engineering aren't separate disciplines in my practice; they're the same job viewed from two ends.
